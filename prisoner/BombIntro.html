Now you will start a new part. On the screen, you will see a field composed of 100 boxes, arranged in a 10 × 10 grid (the numbers on each box will not be visible):

Every two seconds, a box will be collected, starting with Box #1 (top left) and ending with Box #100 (bottom right). You earn 1 point for every box collected. Once collected, the box changes from dark grey to light grey, and your earnings are updated accordingly. At any moment, you can see the number of boxes collected so far and your potential earnings.

Behind one of these boxes, a bomb is hidden that destroys everything collected in this part of the experiment. You do not know the location of the bomb, and even if you collect the bomb you will not be told until the end of the experiment. Your task is to decide when to stop the process by clicking the Stop button at any time.

Payment

  • If, when you hit Stop, none of the boxes you have collected contain the bomb, you receive the total amount accumulated.
  • If, when you hit Stop, you have collected the bomb box, you earn $0.

This means the earnings you see on the screen are only potential earnings. You will earn them only if none of the boxes you collected contained the bomb.

Location of the Bomb

The computer randomly selects a number between 1 and 100, with all numbers equally likely. The bomb is placed in the box with that number.
