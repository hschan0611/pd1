Part 1 consists of a sequence of matches, and each match is made up of several rounds.

Each match will last for several rounds. After each round, there is a 75% probability that the match will continue for another round.

You will interact with the same participant throughout a match.

After a match ends, you will be randomly rematched with another participant for the next match.
