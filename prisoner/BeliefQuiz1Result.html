Reminder of Instructions for Belief Task

To indicate your belief, you will use the slider shown on the screen. The slider represents your assessment of the likelihood (0â€“100%) that the person you are paired with chooses Action 1 or Action 2.

Examples
  • 60%: The person you are paired with has a 60% chance of choosing Action 1.
  • 30%: The person you are paired with has a 30% chance of choosing Action 1.
Payment

In both Part 1 and Part 2, one match will be randomly selected to count toward your payment. A different match will also be selected, and the computer will randomly choose one round from that match to determine your belief-task payment.

Two numbers between 0 and 100 will be drawn independently:

  • If the other person chose Action 1, and your reported probability of Action 1 is higher than either of the two draws, you win 50 points.
  • If the other person chose Action 2, and your reported probability of Action 2 is higher than either draw, you win 50 points.

These rules are designed so that your best strategy is to report your true belief. Your report does not affect the other participant and is not shown to them.
